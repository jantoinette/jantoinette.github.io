Brand Story

Inspired by a hard–of–hearing dancer who choreographs by feeling and memorizing the rhythm of a song on a loudspeaker, mechanical engineer Jeremy conceived this project. He founded Tactus, seeing how the product can help the wider Deaf community and make music more accessible.

Logo Design

With a background in mechanical engineering, the brand carries a level of measured accuracy and reliance on science. The logo came from the idea of incorporating lines of sound wave graphs into the letter “t”. The shape also resembles a dancing figure, which is a respectful nod to the brand story.

Logo color variations

Color Palette

The primary brand color of Tactus is a cool light blue, which evokes trust and professionalism. This is complemented by the warm contrast of a coral accent color, adding energy and vibrancy to the overall design, creating a balanced yet dynamic visual identity.

Typography

Stratos is chosen as the primary typeface, paired with Trade Gothic LT Extended as secondary. The contrasting condensed and extended widths shows that the brand welcomes differences and embraces a wide spectrum of possibilities.
