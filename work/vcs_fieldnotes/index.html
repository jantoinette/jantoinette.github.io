Lecturer: Luiza Dale ↗

Inspired by Mindy Seu's lecture, the Google Calendar platform is manipulated to create a personalized and future-proof website. By deviating from expectations and subverting the system, the project aims to challenge algorithms and protect privacy through queer typography and unconventional visual language.

The online notebook offers different viewing experiences depending on the platform's default Day, Week, Month, Year, Schedule or 4 Day views.
