Challenge

MB has successfully expanded their customer base from 1.6 to 20 million by the end of 2022 following a successful rebrand. With growing customer expectations as well as the emergence of domestic and foreigner competitors in the growing Vietnamese market, MB faces increasing pressure to further innovate and disrupt the status quo.

Solution

Through targeted customer segmentation, strategic growth initiatives, agile UX sprints, and a comprehensive digital banking revamp, we aim to sustain MB's growth trajectory while meeting customers' growing demands and outperforming competitors in the rapidly evolving market.
