Vantage

Year

2021

Project

Fintech Brand refresh

Role

Brand designer @ Prophet

Tool(s)

Adobe Ps, Ai, Figma, Frontify

Through stakeholder interviews, competitor audits, and customer research, we repositioned a multi-asset broker brand as “Vantage” and developed a modern visual identity system incorporating the concept of “Shared Success” and the Fibonacci Golden Ratio. We provided an extensive brand guideline, and a complete redesign of the website and mobile app, reflecting a trusted trading ecosystem.
