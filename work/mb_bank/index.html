Challenge

MB has successfully expanded their customer base from 1.6 to 20 million by the end of 2022 following our successful rebrand. With growing customer expectations as well as the emergence of domestic and foreigner competitors in the growing Vietnamese market, MB faces increasing pressure to further innovate and disrupt the status quo. The challenge was clear: how to sustain growth while ensuring that the bank's offerings remain relevant, user-friendly, and ahead of the curve in a highly competitive market.

Solution

Through our strategic approach of targeted customer segmentation, strategic growth initiatives, agile UX sprints, and a comprehensive digital banking transformation, we were able to deliver a banking experience that is both intuitive and forward-thinking, ensuring MB Bank sustains its growth trajectory while meeting customers' growing demands, and remains a top player in Vietnam’s rapidly evolving financial sector.

User-Centered Design

In collaboration with MB Bank’s product and tech teams, we began a series of agile UX sprints aimed at iterating on the mobile app and online banking platform. Our focus was on simplifying the user interface, improving navigation, and ensuring that the experience was seamless across all devices.

Enhanced Mobile Banking Experience

Mobile banking was a key touchpoint for customers, and we placed particular emphasis on ensuring that the mobile app felt intuitive, fast, and accessible to users of all demographics. Features like account management, money transfers, and bill payments were streamlined for ease of use.

Personalization & Smart Features

We introduced smart features like predictive finance management, budget tracking, and personalized product recommendations based on customer behavior. The app dynamically adjusted to provide each user from different segmentation tiers with relevant banking services, enhancing engagement and user satisfaction.
