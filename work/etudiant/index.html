etudiant

Year

2020

Project

Typeface design

Role

Student @ Type@Cooper

Tool(s)

Robofont, Adobe Id, Leica CL

Etudiant — the gerund form of “to study” in French, also an allophone of étudiant meaning “student” — is the first typeface I designed studying a 10-week course. It is a translation display serif, made by a curious learner who is constantly learning about things in life.
