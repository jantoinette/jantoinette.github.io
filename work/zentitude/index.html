Challenge

While the parent company’s reputation for safety and cutting-edge, eco-conscious technology provides a strong foundation, Zentitude needed to carve out its own niche in the market. The goal is to position the scent line as an exclusive, zen-inspired brand that harmoniously blends technology with nature’s purity, with simplicity as a core principle. The new brand will not only reflect the parent company's values but also establish its own personality that speaks to hygiene, wellness, and sophistication with an attitude.

Proposed symbol concepts: each design reflects a unique approach to capturing the essence of the brand while ensuring versatility and recognizability.
