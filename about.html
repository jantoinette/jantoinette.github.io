I'm Antoinette Chow, a graphic designer living in Hong Kong.

Growing up bilingual, I have always been intruiged by the human exchange of thoughts and ideas.

I believe in the beauty of visual communication, the power to evoke emotions and establish connections, without being restricted by the limited lexicons in spoken or written languages. I always devise a visual language for my designs.

My love for linguistics includes the graphical representations of phonemes.

I taught myself some basic coding and created this website with some help from friends.

I shoot mostly analog on Leica CL, digitally on Fujifilm XT-10, and occassionally a disposible.

Thanks for being here, let's stay in touch!
