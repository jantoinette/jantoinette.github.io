etudiant

typeface design    Type@Cooper

Etudiant — the gerund form of “to study” in French, also an allophone of étudiant meaning “student” — is the first typeface I designed from studying a 10-week course. It is a translation display serif, made by a curious learner who is constantly learning about things in life.
