Hopes & Dreams Club by 823 is an online community created by Ta-ku during the beginning of COVID. This is the third project, where members are to help out a new friend in need, by connecting with another member of the Hopes & Dreams community. Mindy and I used to work together before we got laid off. We coincidentally joined this club and decided to collaborate for this project. We talked about missing each other, and shared slices of our lives through images that are seen in this final video.
