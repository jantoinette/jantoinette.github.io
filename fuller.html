fuller milk

Inspired by the beauty of change in metalworking, this packaging design plays with the contrast between straight rigid lines and organic curvy lines. A fuller is a tool used to form metal when hot. Like how blacksmiths forge metal and give shape to them, fuller is a milk product that provides calcium and strenghtens bones.
