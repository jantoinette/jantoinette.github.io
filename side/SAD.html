Subtle Asian Dating

Year

2019

Project

Facebook post

A Facebook group for members to "auction" their friends for dates to over 560,000 people. Each auction post follows an unofficial template of information including name, age, location, occupation, and personal facts with 5+ photos of the person to be auctioned. In order to stand out from the sea of posts, I've created a 360 photo. The concept is the complexity of the person I was auctioning, that people have to dive in to discover and learn more and will find endearing things about him.
